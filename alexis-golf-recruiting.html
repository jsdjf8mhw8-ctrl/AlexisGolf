About

Meet Alexis

Alexis is a competitive junior golfer with a passion for the game that started at age seven. Over the past decade, she has developed into one of the top junior golfers in her region, competing at both the high school varsity and Junior PGA levels.

Known for her mental composure under pressure and a consistent ball-striking game, Alexis brings the same dedication to her studies that she applies on the course. She is an honor-roll student with a vision of competing at the Division I level while pursuing a degree in sports management or pre-medicine.

Beyond tournament play, Alexis is actively involved in her community through junior golf clinics and serves as a mentor to younger players at her home club.
